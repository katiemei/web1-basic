Wing Leong

Born 28 November, 1924. St. Louis Chinatown. The first son of a young Chinese mother and her much older husband. An American by birth. Chinese by circumstance

A child of the depression. Raised in the basement of the restaurant where his parents worked. Well-fed and pampered. Brother of two. Fatherless by eight. Responsible by nine. Delivery-boy by 14. College graduate by 22. Mechanical Engineer

Married by 29. Father of three. Masters of Engineering. Emerson Electric Aerospace Engineer. Photographer. Traveller. Inquisitive. Curious. Dedicated. Precise. Leader. Intelligent. Memory like a steel trap.

Alone these fats are single unconnected threads. Connected together they weave the tapestry of a life lived fully.

Born into the restaurant business he knew he wanted a different life. Being smart, dedicated to his studies, and having the precise mind of an engineer he set his sights on college and a career.

Lovingly raised and fatherless at a tender age, Wing knew the value of family and married a smart, determined young woman. Together they built and successful family.

Promoted and sent overseas, he rose to the top of his profession while learning about the world around him, never forgetting where he came from.

Retired, active, father, grandfather, and engineer. These are the roles that keep Wing busy and ever present in our lives. Help us celebrate the full tapestry that is Wing's life!
